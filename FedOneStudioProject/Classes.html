CLASSES

Palate Training

Palate training for beginners. Try coffee from around the world in a cupping class. Hone the tongue of a professional. Comes with a take home flavor wheel diagram.

Manual Brew Method 1

A how to course on proper brewing techniques. Water, grind size, filters, and contact time all play in the perfect cup. We focus here on a single brew.

Cold Brew

This cold brew course teaches you how to yield a bigger batch size. Whether for your cafe or your shop you brew it, store it, grab it anytime. Cold brew is always ready to go. Are you?

Manual Brew Method 2

Time to get technical. Take your knowledge from Manual Brew Method 1 and apply it to an array of different brew devices. Aeropress, Chemex, Kalita Wave, French Press. Comes with a recipe card set.

Latte Art 1

Yes, we do that thing you see on the internet. Latte Art. We teach you how to steam milk for espresso drinks for the perfect temperature and the perfect texture. From aerated cappucinos to velety lattes.

Equipment Maintenance

This is a multi class crash course on how to attempt maintenance on your cafe or home brew equipment. You can always have us repair and maintain them here at iBrewLab. But we can help you cut down costs.
